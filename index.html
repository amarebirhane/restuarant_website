About Us

Our Story

Since 1995, Habesha restaurant has been serving exceptional cuisine in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to create dishes that celebrate both tradition and innovation.

We believe that dining is an experience to be savored, shared, and remembered. Every dish is prepared with passion, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence that has made us a beloved destination for food lovers.
